Chicken Bean Salad

As much as i’d like to live my life eating only paranthas & samosas, i have to eat to live and not live to eat. This is where salads come in. Salads are a wonderful way to eat to your fill and not have to worry about counting calories. They’re also an easy and actually tasty way to eat raw vegetables. Dressings do not have to be complicated - a lemon, salt and pepper go a long way. Fresh herbs always give an extra kick. And if spice calls to your soul, fresh finely chopped green chillies can transform any dish. You can be extra cool and add fresh fruits to your salads - pomegranate seeds, orange wedges, persimmons are some great examples. The secret to a good salad is an adequate balance of protein, essential fats and lots and lots of fiber.

For this recipe, you will need:
  • One chicken breast
  • 250ml black beans (canned or boiled)
  • A hard boiled egg
  • Cucumber, halved
  • Tomato, halved
  • Half a cup spinach leaves, washed
  • Onion, thinly sliced
  • Red pepper, thinly chopped
  • Garlic, half a clove
  • Cilantro, for garnishing
  • Green chillies, optional

Instructions

Chicken Bean mixture

  1. Boil 2.5-3 cups of water in a saucepan and add a cube of chicken stock.
  2. Cook the chicken breast completely and allow it to cool.
  3. Saute onions, red peppers and garlic in a tbsp of oil. Add a pinch of salt and black pepper for flavour.
  4. Add a pinch of salt and black pepper for flavour. Shred and toss the cooked meat with the onion pepper mixture.
  5. Drain the black beans from the can (or if you’ve boiled your beans) and add the beans to the chicken pepper mixture.
  6. Sprinkle some taco seasoning - as little or as much as you’d like. You can add any seasoning you fancy

Hard boiled eggs - Perfectly boiled eggs every time

  1. Bring 4-5 cups of water to a boil; add salt to fasten the process.
  2. As the water begins to boil, add eggs.
  3. Once the water is bubbling, cover the saucepan.
  4. Let the eggs cool in the water, on the stove for 25-30 minutes.
  5. Peel the egg and halve it. Sprinkle a pinch of himalayan rock salt and paprika.

Assembly

Serve the chicken bean mixture with tomatoes, cucumber, spinach leaves and avocado. Garnish with cilantro and/or parsley and green onions with a generous squeeze of lemon to top it off. Enjoy!
