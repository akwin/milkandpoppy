Legendary Breakfast

Why legendary? There are many reasons. The first, breakfast, being the first meal of the day is automatically legendary. Second, anything that involves eggs, garlicky spinach and potatoes is heaven to me. This recipe can alsp be subbed in for a legendary brunch - just add orange juice (mimosas anyone?) and upgrade to french toast (stuffed if you're ravenous!)

For two stuffed omelets, You will need:
  • Four eggs
  • Cheese, grated
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach, half a bunch
  • Onion, halved
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green chillies
  • Parsley, to garnish
  • Chilli flakes
  • Pepper, ground
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

You can watch a step-by-step of this recipe on the story highlights on my Instagram. Link in the sidebar.

Whip the eggs with grated cheese and chilli flakes.

Finely chop onions and saute them in a tbsp of butter and oil.

Add chopped ginger, garlic and green chillies. Do not substitute with ginger-garlic paste as that's more appropriate for curries.

Mix in the mushrooms once the onions turn pink.

Add as many spinach as you like - it's going to shrivel anyway!

Add a pinch of salt and pepper to the vegetable and the egg cheese mixture.

as always, add in little amounts - it's always easier to add more later.

Give a good mix to the eggs and pour the mixture on a hot skillet.

Let the omelet cook undistured till it changes color and the top layer is cooked as well. Do not flip the omelet.

Add the vegetable mixture on one side of the omelet and fold the other half over it like a blanket.

Garnish with parsley, green chillies and spring onions. Enjoy!

For roasted potatoes, You will need:
  • Four large potatoes
  • Onion
  • Parsley
  • Spring onions
  • Olive oil
  • Taco seasoning
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Instructions

Step-by-step recipe also available in Instagram story highlights.

Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.

Wash the potatoes well.

Chop them into roughly equal pieces. It doesn't have to be perfect but try to keep them more or less the same size.

In an oven safe dish, mix the potatoes in olive oil.

Add the chopped onion, parsley and spring onions

Mix in the taco seasoning, garlic powder, salt and pepper well.

Cook the potatoes in the oven for 25-30 minutes or until golden brown

Generously add grated cheese and let the heat of the potatoes melt it.

Garnish with parsley, green chillies, spring onions and your favorite seasoning.
