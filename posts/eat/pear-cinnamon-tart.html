Pear Cinnamon Tart

Welcomed the New Year with this sweet and tart dessert. The puff pastry is flaky and melts in your mouth. The coarse sugar crunches in the mouth and gives the dish a unique texture. The pears are fragrant, sour and sweet. So many flavours that work surprisingly well together.

You will need:
  • Four pears
  • One pack of puff pastry
  • Half a cup or 125ml butter
  • Coarse sugar, three-four tsp
  • Cinnamon, a pinch
  • Honey, to garnish

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 300 degrees.

Cut fresh pears into thin slices.

Mix in coarse sugar and keep the pear slices aside.



Roll out the two parts of the puff pastry into one big rectangle.

Press down on the edges using a fork to create a pretty design.






Butter your baking sheet.


Transfer the rolled out puff pastry onto the baking sheet and add more butter!




Put the sugared pears onto the pastry.

Sprinkle cinnamon generously.




Bake the tart for 15-25 minutes - keep an eye on the puff pastry and when it starts to brown, this great dessert is ready! Enjoy with a drizzle of honey.
